The Summer Signature Series comprises four events, taking place in May, June, July, and August. Each event will recognise an individual winner, determined by the highest individual net Stableford score of the day.

  • 0 Points: Double Bogey (+2) or Worse
  • 1 Point: Bogey (+1)
  • 2 Points: Par (E)
  • 3 Points: Birdie (-1)
  • 4 Points: Eagle (-2)
  • 5 Points: Albatross (-3) or Better

At the conclusion of the series, an overall Series Champion will be announced. This title will go to the golfer who accumulates the most points across the series, based on their best four scores from a possible six rounds. For instance, if a participant plays all six rounds, only their top four scores will contribute to the overall standings.

A $20 cash entry fee applies to each event. Half of the fee will be awarded to the winner of the individual event, while the remaining half will be added to the cumulative prize fund for the Series Champion.
