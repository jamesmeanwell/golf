Island Golf Tour

It is with great pleasure that I present to you the Island Golf Tour—a modest assembly in number, yet grand in spirit, devoted to the timeless pursuit of the game we hold in such high esteem. As Ben Hogan observed, golf is a game of precision and patience, a quiet contest not only against the course, but within oneself. In that same vein, we gather not merely to keep score, but to test our resolve, sharpen our character, and share in the fellowship that has long defined this noble pastime.

Sam Snead spoke of golf as a game for a lifetime, while Bobby Jones reminded us that its true measure lies not in trophies, but in the integrity with which it is played. And so, in the tradition of Old Tom Morris and the great custodians of the game, may this tour be conducted with honor, camaraderie, and a deep respect for the enduring spirit of golf.
