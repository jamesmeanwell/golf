This season-long challenge at Caughnawaga Golf Club is a net stroke play competition. Gentlemen may complete as many rounds as they wish throughout the season. At the conclusion of the season, the gentleman with the lowest recorded net round will be declared the winner.

To enter a score, competitors must submit a photograph of their scorecard along with their handicap. Only a competitor’s lowest net score will be recorded for the competition standings. For example, if a competitor records an 83 in one round and, on a later attempt, returns a 76, both scores may be submitted, with only the lower score retained. Conversely, if a competitor later records a higher score—such as an 85—that round need not be submitted, as it will not improve his position.
